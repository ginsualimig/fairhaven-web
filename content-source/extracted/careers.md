# Investor Relations Associate


#### Position Overview

As an Investor Relations Associate at Fairhaven Property Group, you will play a vital role in supporting our Investor Relations Manager in building and maintaining strong relationships with our investors. This entry-level position requires excellent people skills, a comfort with cold calling and reaching out to new contacts, and the ability to manage various tasks as directed by the Investor Relations Manager. You will work closely with our CRM and assist in reporting, communications, and other investor-related activities. This is a hybrid role, requiring occasional office meetings and offering flexibility in your work environment.

Location: Hybrid - Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia / Remote


#### Responsibilities

1. Investor Support:

- Assist the Investor Relations Manager with all investor relations activities, providing exceptional service and addressing inquiries and concerns.
- Develop and maintain strong relationships with existing and potential investors, fostering long-term partnerships.
- Manage investor communications, including newsletters, reports, and updates on fund performance and investment opportunities.
- Assist in organizing investor events, conferences, and meetings to promote investor engagement and cultivate a sense of community.
2. Outreach and Engagement:

- Conduct cold calls and reach out to new potential investors, introducing them to Fairhaven Property Group and our offerings.
- Work with the Investor Relations Manager to identify and engage with target investor segments, leveraging your communication skills to build connections.
3. Reporting and CRM Management:

- Manage reporting to the Investor Relations Manager, ensuring accurate and timely information is provided.
- Utilize our CRM (HubSpot preferred) to track and manage investor interactions, ensuring all data is up-to-date and accurately recorded.
4. Administrative Support:

- Assist with administrative tasks related to investor relations, including scheduling meetings, preparing presentations, and organizing documentation.
- Collaborate with internal teams to gather information and insights to support investor relations efforts.

#### Requirements

- Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Marketing, Business Administration, or a related field.
- Entry-level position; experience in sales or finance is a plus.
- Proficiency with CRM systems (HubSpot preferred) and Microsoft Office Suite.
- Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to build and maintain relationships with diverse stakeholders.
- Strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.
- Adaptability and a willingness to learn and take on new challenges.
- This is a hybrid role, with occasional office meetings required.
- Strong people skills and the ability to understand and connect with investors are highly important.

#### We offer

- Fantastic career development options
- Rewards for initiative
- An autonomous, self-managing position.
This job description provides a general overview of the responsibilities and requirements for the Investor Relations Associate. Fairhaven Property Group is a growing organization, and as such, additional roles and responsibilities may arise based on the needs of the company. The successful candidate will have the opportunity to contribute to various aspects of the business, making a meaningful impact and supporting the overall success of Fairhaven Property Group.

Location: Hybrid - Dunedin, Otago, New Zealand / Remote

# Executive Assistant


#### Position Overview

Fairhaven Property Group is seeking a highly motivated and organized individual to join our team as an Executive Assistant. As an Executive Assistant, you will provide comprehensive support to the Managing Partner and Investor Relations Managers, ensuring smooth operations and effective communication within the organization. Your role will be diverse and dynamic, requiring excellent organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously.

Location: Hybrid - Dunedin, Otago, New Zealand / Remote


#### Responsibilities

- Investor Onboarding : Assist with gathering details from investors to facilitate the onboarding process, including documentation, account setup, and communications. Maintain accurate and up-to-date investor records.
- Email and Correspondence Management : Monitor and manage the Managing Partner's and Investor Relations Managers' email accounts, draft responses, and prioritize incoming messages. Ensure timely and professional communication with internal and external stakeholders.
- Schedule and Calendar Management : Coordinate and manage schedules, appointments, and meetings for the Managing Partner and Investor Relations Manager. Prepare meeting agendas, documentation, and follow-up materials as necessary.
- Administrative Support : Provide general administrative support to the team, including organizing meetings, preparing reports and presentations, maintaining office supplies, and managing office equipment and facilities.
- Data Management : Assist in organizing and maintaining databases, tracking systems, and other relevant information to support effective investor relations and operational efficiency.
- Project Coordination : Collaborate with various departments to support and coordinate cross-functional projects. Assist in project management, including tracking deadlines, deliverables, and progress.
- Documentation and Filing : Maintain accurate and organized electronic and physical filing systems. Ensure proper documentation and filing of confidential and sensitive information.
- Additional Responsibilities : As a valued member of our team, you may be called upon to assist in various other tasks and responsibilities to support the smooth functioning of Fairhaven Property Group. These may include marketing support, event coordination, research projects, and other ad hoc duties as needed.

#### Requirements

- Proven experience as an Executive Assistant or similar administrative role.
- Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
- Strong organizational and time management skills, with the ability to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines.
- Attention to detail and a high level of accuracy in work.
- Proficient computer skills, including proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).
- Ability to handle confidential and sensitive information with discretion.
- Strong interpersonal skills, with the ability to work collaboratively and effectively in a team environment.
- Adaptability and flexibility to navigate a fast-paced and dynamic work environment.
- Proactive and self-motivated with the ability to take initiative and solve problems.
- Knowledge of the real estate industry or experience in investor relations is a plus.

# Investor Relations Manager


### Position Overview

As the Investor Relations Manager at Fairhaven Property Group, you will play a critical and dynamic role in building and maintaining strong relationships with our investors. This position requires expertise in investor relations, branding, marketing, and the ability to contribute to various aspects of our growing organization. You will have the opportunity to work closely with investors in South East Asia and Oceania, leveraging your regional knowledge and networks to support our strategic goals. As a member of our small and collaborative team, you will also be expected to wear multiple hats and contribute to various initiatives beyond investor relations.

Location: Hybrid - Dunedin, Otago, New Zealand / Remote


#### Responsibilities

1. Investor Relations:

- Serve as the primary point of contact for investors in South East Asia and Oceania, providing exceptional service and addressing their inquiries and concerns.
- Develop and maintain strong relationships with existing and potential investors, ensuring their satisfaction and fostering long-term partnerships.
- Coordinate and facilitate investor communications, including newsletters, reports, and updates on fund performance and investment opportunities.
- Organize investor events, conferences, and meetings to promote investor engagement and cultivate a sense of community.
2. Branding and Marketing:

- Collaborate with the marketing team to develop and execute branding strategies that align with the company's vision and values.
- Contribute to the development of marketing materials, presentations, and collateral to effectively communicate the value proposition and differentiate Fairhaven Property Group in the market.
- Assist in ensuring consistent messaging and branding across all investor-facing communications, both online and offline.
- Support market research and analysis efforts to identify target investor segments, market trends, and competitive positioning.
- Contribute to enhancing the online presence through website content, social media, and other digital marketing initiatives.
3. Reporting and Analytics:

- Prepare regular investor reports and presentations to provide insights into fund performance, market trends, and investment strategies.
- Utilize data and analytics to track and measure the effectiveness of investor relations and marketing initiatives.
- Monitor and analyze investor feedback, market trends, and competitor activities to identify opportunities for improvement and innovation.
4. Additional Responsibilities:

- Contribute to the development and implementation of investor relations and marketing strategies, leveraging your knowledge and experience in the South East Asia and Oceania markets.
- Assist in organizing and coordinating investor roadshows, conferences, and other investor-related events.
- Collaborate with internal teams to gather information and insights to support investor relations and marketing efforts.
- Contribute to the growth and development of the investor relations function within a small and dynamic team.
- Assist with other company-related tasks as needed, demonstrating flexibility and a willingness to contribute to various initiatives within a small team.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, such as finance, operations, and legal, to support the overall growth and success of Fairhaven Property Group.
- Provide insights and recommendations based on industry knowledge and investor feedback to support strategic decision-making across the organization.
- Stay updated on industry trends, regulations, and best practices related to investor relations and marketing, sharing relevant information with the team.
- Act as a brand ambassador for Fairhaven Property Group, promoting our values, culture, and investment opportunities to internal and external stakeholders.

#### Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Marketing, Business Administration, or a related field. MBA or CFA designation is a plus.
- Proven experience in investor relations, branding, marketing, and working with investors in South East Asia and Oceania, preferably within the financial services or real estate industry.
- Strong knowledge of investment products, capital markets, and investor relations best practices.
- Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to build and maintain relationships with diverse stakeholders.
- Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to effectively convey complex investment concepts to both sophisticated and non-sophisticated investors.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to analyze market data and trends to inform decision-making.
- Proficiency in digital marketing tools, CRM systems, and Microsoft Office Suite.
- Detail-oriented, organized, and able to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced and collaborative environment.
