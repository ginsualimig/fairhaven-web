# Charity Initiatives

Our giving initiatives through B1G1 are carefully chosen to align with our core values and mission. We focus on causes that promote education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and poverty alleviation. By supporting these initiatives, we strive to make a meaningful and sustainable difference in the lives of individuals and communities around the world.

Through our partnership with B1G1, we have witnessed the transformative power of collective giving. Together, we have been able to support initiatives such as building schools, providing clean water and sanitation facilities, supporting medical treatments, and empowering disadvantaged communities with education and vocational training.

At Fairhaven Property Group, we are proud to partner with B1G1 (Buy1Give1), a global giving initiative that allows us to make a meaningful impact in the world. Through our collaboration with B1G1, we have the opportunity to support charitable causes and create positive change in communities globally.

With B1G1, every business transaction we undertake has the power to make a difference. Through a unique giving model, we contribute a portion of our revenue to a wide range of impactful projects and charities. By doing so, we aim to address pressing social and environmental challenges and create a better future for all.

We firmly believe that businesses have a responsibility to give back and contribute to the greater good. By integrating giving into our everyday operations, we are able to create a positive ripple effect, inspiring others to join us in making a difference.

As part of our commitment to transparency and accountability, we regularly share updates and stories about the impact of our giving initiatives. Together, we can create a world where everyone has access to the resources and opportunities they need to thrive.


### Join us in our journey of giving and together, let's make a lasting impact that extends far beyond the boundaries of our business.
