### Discretionary Fund Management

Introducing Fairhaven Property Group’s inaugural discretionary fund management portfolio, tailored for wholesale investors in pursuit of dynamic growth. We project returns yielding 15%* net profit after tax and fees annually. This fund is crafted for wholesale investors with a moderate risk appetite, employing a dynamic strategy centered on acquiring and enhancing high-potential land and commercial properties for substantial medium to long-term gains. It provides investors with a strategic opportunity not only for diversification and resilience in their portfolios but also for broad exposure across various sectors within New Zealand's vibrant real estate market.

We excel in dealflow and deal sourcing through our extensive network, market insights, and strategic partnerships across New Zealand, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, and Southeast Asia. Actively monitoring the market, we identify high-potential real estate and investment opportunities, often securing deals before they go public. With rigorous due diligence and financial analysis, we ensure each opportunity aligns with our investors' goals. This proactive, data-driven approach enables us to offer exclusive, high-return investments, delivering well-structured deals that generate long-term value and growth for our partners.
