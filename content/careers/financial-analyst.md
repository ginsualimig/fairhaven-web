# Financial Analyst


### Position Overview

The Financial Analyst role at Fairhaven Property Group offers an exceptional opportunity for individuals seeking to launch their careers in the real estate investment industry. As a key member of our dynamic team, you will contribute to our investment activities by conducting comprehensive financial analysis, assisting in investment valuation, and supporting data-driven decision-making processes. You will play a crucial role in conducting research, preparing insightful financial reports, and collaborating with cross-functional teams. This position provides valuable exposure to the real estate industry and the opportunity to develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Location: Hybrid - Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia / Remote


#### Responsibilities

- Demonstrate a proactive and self-driven attitude to take on new challenges and learn on the  job.
- Conduct thorough financial analysis, including financial modeling, investment valuation, and  risk assessment, to evaluate potential investment opportunities in various property sectors.
- Research and gather data from diverse sources to support financial analysis and  decision-making processes.
- Collaborate with team members to identify areas for process improvement, efficiency gains,  and innovative solutions.
- Assist in preparing financial reports and presentations to communicate findings, insights, and  recommendations to stakeholders.
- Continuously stay updated on industry trends, market conditions, and best practices in  financial analysis and real estate investment.
- Utilize available resources and seek out new learning opportunities to enhance skills and  knowledge in financial analysis and real estate investment.
- Proactively identify and address potential issues or risks in investment projects and propose  solutions.
- Contribute to the development and improvement of financial models, forecasting tools, and  investment evaluation processes.
- Support cross-functional teams and collaborate on special projects as assigned.

#### Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to think critically and find creative  solutions.
- Excellent research skills and the ability to gather and analyze data from various sources.  - Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and other financial analysis tools.
- Self-motivated and eager to learn, with a proactive approach to tackling challenges.
- Strong communication skills and the ability to effectively convey complex financial concepts.
- Detail-oriented and highly organized, with the ability to manage multiple tasks and meet  deadlines.
- Adaptability and flexibility to thrive in a fast-paced and dynamic work environment.
- Knowledge of real estate markets and investment principles is a plus.
At Fairhaven Property Group, we value resourcefulness, innovation, and a commitment to  excellence. We offer a competitive compensation package, including salary and benefits, along with ample opportunities for professional growth and development. This is an entry-level position ideal for individuals seeking to launch their careers in the real estate investment industry. To apply, please submit your resume and a cover letter outlining your qualifications, highlighting your resourcefulness and ability to adapt in challenging situations.
